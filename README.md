# Global Water Fund

**Global Water Fund** is a nonprofit organization advancing strategic intelligence, diplomacy, and finance pathways for water security as a pillar of human and national security.

This repository hosts the public website for **globalwaterfund.org**, built as a lightweight, transparent platform to support collaboration with universities, governments, policy institutions, and investors.

---

## Purpose

Global Water Fund focuses on:
- Translating water risk into decision-grade strategic intelligence  
- Convening discreet, high-trust dialogues across policy, security, and finance  
- Supporting pathways from analysis to implementation and investment
