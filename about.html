About

An institute built for this century.

Zurich-based, globally networked. Focused on water as the foundation of human and national security.

The Global Water Fund Institute, led by Stella Thomas, works with a high-level network of Heads of State, industry leaders, investors, philanthropists, and leading universities to address the world’s water challenges.

Through research, writing, and convening, the Institute supports national water strategies, strengthens institutional capacity, promotes regional governance, and advances finance pathways for resilient economies.
