Purpose

In an era of technological achievement and capital accumulation, too many still lack clean water and reliable energy. Water remains the decisive constraint—and the decisive opportunity—for stability, growth, and legitimacy.

The Global Water Fund Institute, led by Stella Thomas, is a Zurich-based institute working with a high-level network of policymakers, industry leaders, investors, philanthropists, and universities to address the world’s water challenges.

We focus on water as a strategic variable shaping economic resilience, governance, and national security—translating complexity into clarity leaders can use.
